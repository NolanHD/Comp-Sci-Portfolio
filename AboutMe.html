What are your 2 favorite books?

My two favorite books are "The Boys in the Boat" and "I am the Messenger"

        Why do you like them?

Both of these books are both great, but they are great in their own way. The Boys in the Boat is a book perserverance and finding yourself in the face of adversity. The book I am the Messenger is more of a mystery novel but is at the time happy and uplifting.
